Anchor Check Ins — Teacher Guide

Android · Summer VBS · read-only class roster

Install link (open on your Android phone):
https://play.google.com/apps/internaltest/4701595538991636552

Sign in: same email & password as https://events.ipchouston.com
Program: Summer VBS (your account is already scoped to this event)

1. Install (one time)

  1. On your Android phone, open the install link above in Chrome.
  2. Sign in with the Gmail your admin added as a tester.
  3. Tap Become a tester, then Install.
  4. Open Anchor Check Ins from your app list.

2. Sign in

  1. Enter your church staff email and password.
  2. If asked to pick a program, choose Summer VBS.
  3. You’ll land on Home or My class.

3. What you’ll use

  • My class — your roster and who is checked in today
  • Home — today’s checked-in / expected counts for your class
  • News — announcements from VBS leaders
  • More — profile and sign out

You cannot check students in, scan QR codes, or print badges — view only.

4. Viewing your class

  1. Tap the My class tab at the bottom.
  2. See each student and whether they’re In or Expected for today.
  3. Tap a name for details (allergies, guardian contact — read only).
  4. Multi-day VBS: use the day chips at the top to switch days.

5. Quick help

  • Not a tester? Ask admin to add your Gmail to the Play test list.
  • Wrong / empty class? Admin must assign you as class leader for Summer VBS.
  • Can’t sign in? Reset password on the website first.
  • Need an update? Play Store → Anchor Check Ins → Update.

Questions? Contact your VBS coordinator.
