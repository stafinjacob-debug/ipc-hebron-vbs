1. Install (one time)

  1. Install TestFlight from the App Store (free, by Apple).
  2. On your iPhone or iPad, open the TestFlight link above in Safari.
  3. Tap Accept, then Install for Anchor Check Ins.
  4. Open the app from your home screen (orange dot = beta via TestFlight).

2. Sign in

  1. Enter your church staff email and password.
  2. If asked to pick a program, choose Summer VBS.
  3. You’ll land on Home or My class.

3. What you’ll use

  • My class — your roster and who is checked in today
  • Home — today’s checked-in / expected counts for your class
  • News — announcements from VBS leaders
  • More — profile and sign out

You cannot check students in, scan QR codes, or print badges — view only.

4. Viewing your class

  1. Tap the My class tab at the bottom.
  2. See each student as In or Expected for today.
  3. Tap a name for details (allergies, guardian contact — read only).
  4. Multi-day VBS: use the day chips at the top to switch days.

5. Updates & help

  • New version? Open TestFlight → Anchor Check Ins → Update.
  • Wrong / empty class? Admin must assign you as class leader for Summer VBS.
  • Can’t sign in? Reset password on the website first.
  • Link won’t open? Use Safari on the device; install TestFlight first.

Questions? Contact your VBS coordinator.
