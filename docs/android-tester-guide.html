Anchor Check Ins

Android app — guide for internal testers (staff, volunteers & teachers)

App name: Anchor Check Ins

Who this is for: IPC Hebron VBS staff with a login on the admin website

Internal test invite link:
https://play.google.com/apps/internaltest/4701595538991636552

Website (same login): https://events.ipchouston.com

Part 1 — Install the app (one-time setup)

Important: You must use an Android phone or tablet and the same Google account your church admin added to the tester list. The link will not work on iPhone/iPad (use TestFlight for Apple devices).
1
Open the invite link on your Android device.
Tap or paste this link in Chrome (recommended):
https://play.google.com/apps/internaltest/4701595538991636552
2
Sign in to Google if prompted. Use the Gmail account your admin registered for testing.
3
Become a tester. On the Play Store page, tap Become a tester (or Accept invite), then confirm.
4
Install the app. Tap Download it on Google Play or Install. Wait for the download to finish.
5
Open Anchor Check Ins from your home screen or app drawer. The icon may show the church logo once the Play Store listing is fully reviewed; the app name is Anchor Check Ins.
Can’t install? Ask your admin to confirm your Gmail address is on the internal tester list. Uninstall any old test copy first, then open the invite link again.

Part 2 — Sign in & choose your VBS program

1
Sign in with the same email and password you use on the VBS admin website (https://events.ipchouston.com).
2
Choose your VBS season/program if asked. Pick the current year’s VBS event. If you only have access to one program, the app may select it automatically.
3
Face ID / fingerprint lock (optional). You can turn on biometric lock under More → Security so the app asks for your fingerprint when you return to it.
If you see “No program assigned”, contact your admin. They need to assign your account to the correct VBS program under Settings → Users on the website.

Part 3 — What you see (depends on your role)

Your role What you can do in the app Main tabs
Teacher / class leader View your class roster, see who is checked in today, view student details (read-only). You cannot check students in or print badges. Home, My class, News, More
Check-in volunteer Scan QR codes, search by name, check students in/out, print badges (on supported iPad stations; Android may use system print where available). Home, Check-In, Classes, News, More
Admin / registration staff Full check-in desk access, all classes, announcements, and dashboard counts for the day. All tabs

Part 4 — Using the app day-to-day

Home

  • Shows Checked in and Expected counts for today’s camp day.
  • Lists your classes with today’s attendance.
  • Teachers: quick link to My class (no check-in button).

My class / Classes (teachers & staff)

  • Tap a student to view their details.
  • For multi-day VBS, use the day chips at the top to switch camp days.
  • Teachers with one class stay on the roster tab — use the bottom tabs to switch screens.

Check-In (volunteers & admins only)

  1. Open the Check-In tab.
  2. Scan a family’s QR code or search by child name / registration number.
  3. Confirm the match and tap to check in.
  4. Print a badge if your station is configured for printing.

News

  • Read season announcements from your VBS leaders.

More

  • View your profile and role.
  • Switch VBS season (if you have access to more than one program).
  • Sign out when you are done.

Part 5 — Troubleshooting

Problem What to try
Invite link says I’m not a tester Confirm the correct Gmail is signed in on the device. Ask admin to add your email to the internal tester list.
Sign-in fails Verify email/password on the website first. Password reset is done on the website, not in the app.
“No program assigned” Admin must assign your user to the VBS program (and class, if you’re a teacher) on the website.
Wrong class or empty roster Confirm you’re on the correct VBS season (More → Switch season). Teachers: admin must link you as class leader.
App won’t load data Check Wi‑Fi or cellular data. Pull down to refresh. Force-quit and reopen the app.
Need an update Open the Play Store → Anchor Check Ins → Update. Internal testers receive updates through the same Play listing.

Need help?

Contact your VBS tech coordinator or church admin. Have ready: your login email, role (teacher / volunteer / admin), device type (Android), and a screenshot of any error message.
