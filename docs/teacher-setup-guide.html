Use the same email and password everywhere. Your account is scoped to Summer VBS.
Do this first: Everyone should complete Part 1 (portal invite) before installing the app. Pick Part 2 or Part 3 depending on your phone — you only need one.

Part 1 — Redeem your portal invite (everyone)

An invitation email has been sent to your church email address. You need to activate your account once before you can sign in on the website or in the app.

  1. Open the email with subject “IPC Hebron VBS — You’re invited to the VBS staff portal” (check spam/junk if you don’t see it).
  2. Tap the blue Accept invitation button in the email.
    If the button doesn’t work, copy the full link from the email and paste it into your browser.
  3. On the Accept your invitation page, create a password (at least 8 characters) and confirm it.
  4. Tap Activate account.
  5. When you see “Your account is ready,” tap Go to sign in (or go to https://events.ipchouston.com).
  6. Sign in with your email and the password you just created.

Already activated? If you completed this step before, skip to Part 2 or 3 and sign in with your existing password.

  • Link expired or invalid? The invite link expires after 7 days. Contact your VBS coordinator to resend the invite.
  • Forgot your password? Use the password reset option on the sign-in page at https://events.ipchouston.com.

Part 2 — iPhone / iPad (TestFlight)

Use an iPhone or iPad for this section. Android users should skip to Part 3.

Install (one time)

  1. Install TestFlight from the App Store (free, by Apple).
  2. On your iPhone or iPad, open this link in Safari:
    https://testflight.apple.com/join/btws7xe4
  3. Tap Accept, then Install for Anchor Check Ins.
  4. Open the app from your home screen (orange dot = beta via TestFlight).

Sign in & use the app

  1. Enter the same email and password you set up in Part 1.
  2. If asked to pick a program, choose Summer VBS.
  3. Use these tabs:
    • My class — your roster; who is checked in today
    • Home — today’s checked-in / expected counts for your class
    • News — announcements from VBS leaders
    • More — profile and sign out

Teachers cannot check students in, scan QR codes, or print badges — view only.

  • Tap a student’s name on My class for details (allergies, guardian contact).
  • Multi-day VBS: use the day chips at the top to switch days.
  • New version? Open TestFlight → Anchor Check Ins → Update.
  • Link won’t open? Use Safari on the device; install TestFlight first.

Part 3 — Android phone (Google Play internal test)

Use an Android phone for this section. You must sign in with the Gmail account your admin added as a tester. iPhone/iPad users should use Part 2 instead.

Install (one time)

  1. On your Android phone, open this link in Chrome:
    https://play.google.com/apps/internaltest/4701595538991636552
  2. Sign in with the Gmail your admin added as a tester, if prompted.
  3. Tap Become a tester, then confirm.
  4. Tap Download it on Google Play or Install.
  5. Open Anchor Check Ins from your app list.

Sign in & use the app

  1. Enter the same email and password you set up in Part 1.
  2. If asked to pick a program, choose Summer VBS.
  3. Use these tabs:
    • My class — your roster; who is checked in today
    • Home — today’s checked-in / expected counts for your class
    • News — announcements from VBS leaders
    • More — profile and sign out

Teachers cannot check students in, scan QR codes, or print badges — view only.

  • Tap a student’s name on My class for details (allergies, guardian contact).
  • Multi-day VBS: use the day chips at the top to switch days.
  • Not a tester? Ask your VBS coordinator to add your Gmail to the Play test list.
  • Need an update? Play Store → Anchor Check Ins → Update.

Help

  • Wrong or empty class? Your VBS coordinator must assign you as class leader for Summer VBS.
  • Can’t sign in? Complete Part 1 first, or reset your password on the website.
  • “No program assigned”? Contact your VBS coordinator — your account needs Summer VBS access.
